Joshua Sobel

Organist. Recording Engineer. Educator.

ABOUT ME

Joshua Sobel has been an avid musician for nearly two decades. He currently serves as organist at Centenary UMC in Winston-Salem, NC, and is Co-Artistic Director of the Maurice Duruflé Academy of Music. As a recording engineer, he has produced recordings for, among others, the American Guild of Organists, the Organ Historical Society, and the Oxford University Music Press.

RECENT WORK

I am honored to have been asked to record the Piedmont Wind Symphony's winter/holiday concert in December 2023. This was an especially interesting experience, as it was my first time using on-site recording gear for a large instrumental ensemble, with a very limited time for set-up. The resulting recording of the whole concert was broadcast on WTOB.
